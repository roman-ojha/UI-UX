*) The Website Header:
    -> A website header usually the top section of a webpage that contains the logo, navigation menu, a call-to-action and optionally a search form.
    -> A header is highly customizable and can host other types of content.
    -> Headers can also be vertical.
    -> EX:

        -> https://www.adidas.com/us
