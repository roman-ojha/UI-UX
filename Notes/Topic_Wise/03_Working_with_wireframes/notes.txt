*) Wireframes:
    -> These are low fidelity versions of you final design.
    -> like a Sketches.
    -> It represent or it shows the final content of the project also it's structure.
    -> A wireframe should be devoid of any intentional aesthetic decision like color or typography.
    -> In wireframes we don't care about colors, typefaces, spacing, sizing.
    -> Wireframe is purely representing the content and the structure of that content.
    -> This comes under the UX part.
