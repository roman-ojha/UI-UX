*) The Footer:
    -> The website's footer is an area positioned at the bottom of every page on a website, right bellow the main content.
    -> Footers usually contain information that improves the usability of the website.
    -> Footer also display content that doesn't belong anywhere else, like copyright text, external links and sitemaps.
